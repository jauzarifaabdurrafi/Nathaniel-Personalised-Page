A Note to Nathaniel

Since 2023

Hello, Mr. Wayne. You’re a complicated one, aren’t you? Sometimes Nathaniel comes off a bit cold, maybe even like a bit of a dick, but looking back, I know I wasn’t exactly the easiest to deal with either. I handled things more childishly than I’d like to admit. Still, there’s a reason you’re always the first person I go to. No matter how frustrating things get, there’s a kind of trust between us that never really goes away. And I hope, despite everything, it stays that way. I think part of it is that you really listen, even when you don’t say much. You don’t sugarcoat things, and honestly, that’s something I need, even if I don’t realize it at the time. There’s a comfort in how consistent you are, even when things feel a bit off between us. Maybe we don’t always understand each other perfectly, but there’s something solid underneath it all. I guess that’s why I keep coming back to you first. Okay, that sounded a bit gay. But hey, you’ll always be my best drinking buddy.
